the warranty and procedures policy

• Window film care instructions:

If there is a foggy appearance after installation: This condition is temporary and results from the moisture used in installing the film. After that moisture dries, this foggy appearance will disappear. Do not operate the windows until the film dries. The car window tinting film from 3M can be washed with known washing solutions. Thirty (30) days after installation. Do not use abrasive cleaning agents or rough brushes that may scratch the film.

• Care instructions:

#You can wash the car as usual using a little water and soap twenty-four hours after installing the film. 3M recommends using 3M car wash soap to wash the car. #Remove any foreign objects (eg insects, bird droppings, etc.) as soon as possible to avoid permanent stains. #If a high-pressure washer is used, the nozzle should be 36 feet away from the edges of the film to avoid unraveling the film. #After waxing the car, you can remove any wax buildup along the edges of the film with a soft cloth, and 3M recommends using a 3M microfiber cloth to clean cars. #Use wax that does not contain dyes, chemicals, or rubbing compounds. 3M recommends using the 3M Perforation Finish product when waxing the car.
